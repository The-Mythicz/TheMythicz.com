Mythology Researcher

Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Apply

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Mythology, History, or related field
  • 2 years of experience in research and writing
  • Knowledge of Indian mythology

Preferred Qualifications:

  • PhD in Mythology or related field
  • Experience working with cultural consultants
  • Ability to adapt myths for modern storytelling

About the Job:

  • Conduct in-depth research into mythologies, legends, folklore, and religious traditions relevant to the game’s theme or setting.
  • Ensure the myths and their adaptations are presented with sensitivity to their cultural origins.
  • Identify connections or thematic similarities between myths to enrich storytelling.
  • Provide source material for creating compelling storylines, characters, and settings based on mythological elements.
  • Help design characters inspired by mythical figures, ensuring they align with the lore while fitting the game’s style.
  • Assist in constructing immersive worlds influenced by mythological themes, including architecture, creatures, and traditions.
  • Advise on the ethical adaptation of mythological content, avoiding misrepresentation or oversimplification.
  • Work with cultural consultants and scholars to ensure accurate and respectful use of source material.
  • Ensure that mythological elements used are either public domain or adapted in compliance with copyright laws.
  • Navigate the ethical use of myths from cultures with living traditions and spiritual practices.
