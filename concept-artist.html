Narrative Designers

Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Apply

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Proven experience in creative writing, storytelling, or scriptwriting, preferably for games or interactive media.
  • Ability to craft compelling characters, dialogue, and story arcs tailored to gameplay.
  • Excellent teamwork skills to work effectively with designers, artists, and developers.
  • Familiarity with game mechanics and how narrative integrates into gameplay.
  • Experience with narrative design tools (e.g., Twine, Ink, or similar) and game engines like Unity or Unreal.
  • Ability to write for diverse tones, genres, and audiences.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • A robust portfolio showcasing relevant narrative work.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's Degree in Animation, Fine Arts, Graphic Design, Illustration, or related fields.

Responsibilities:

  • Create and refine the overarching narrative, including plotlines, themes, and character arcs.
  • Develop compelling characters with unique backstories, personalities, and motivations.
  • Craft engaging and authentic dialogue for characters, NPCs, and in-game interactions.
  • Design the lore, history, and rules of the game world to create an immersive experience.
  • Collaborate with game designers and storyboard artists to ensure the story aligns seamlessly with gameplay mechanics and objectives
  • Write scripts for cutscenes, in-game events, and cinematics.
  • Develop branching storylines and consequences for player decisions.
  • Oversee the implementation of story elements, including voiceover scripts, environmental storytelling, and text descriptions.
  • Work closely with artists, level designers, and voice actors to bring the narrative to life.
  • Ensure consistency in tone, style, and lore across all narrative elements.
  • Focus on creating emotionally resonant and memorable storytelling moments that enhance player immersion.
  • Maintain detailed narrative bibles and documentation for reference by the entire development team.
