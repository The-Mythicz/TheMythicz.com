Concept Artists

Hyderabad, Telangana, India

Apply

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in fine arts, illustration, animation, graphic design, or a related field.
  • A well-organized portfolio showcasing a variety of skills such as character design, environment design, props, and creatures.
  • Proficiency in Art Software (Adobe Photoshop, Corel Painter, SketchUp, ZBrush, Blender or Autodesk Maya)
  • Knowledge of traditional drawing and painting techniques can be a plus for concept art.
  • Knowledge of color theory, lighting, perspective, and composition to create compelling visuals.
  • Understanding how to design environments that are visually engaging and serve the gameplay or narrative.
  • Experience in game development, illustration, or other design work can be important (including internship experience).
  • The ability to present and explain designs to team members and stakeholders clearly.
  • Meeting deadlines and working efficiently under pressure is essential in the gaming industry.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's Degree in Animation, Fine Arts, Graphic Design, Illustration, or related fields.
  • Skills in traditional mediums like pencil, ink, or paint.
  • Ability to create storyboards or visual sequences.
  • Sensitivity to diverse cultures for creating inclusive and globally appealing designs.

Responsibilities:

  • Translate game ideas and narrative descriptions into compelling visual concepts. Collaborate with game designers and writers to bring characters, environments, and objects to life.
  • Develop high-quality sketches, illustrations, and digital paintings to define the look and feel of the game. Produce artwork for characters, creatures, environments, props, and vehicles.
  • Work closely with 3D modelers, animators, and level designers to ensure the art style is consistent and achievable. Coordinate with art directors to align designs with the game’s vision and style guide.
  • Research historical, cultural, and thematic references to inform designs. Stay updated on trends in gaming and art to ensure innovative and fresh concepts.
  • Revise and refine artwork based on feedback from directors, stakeholders, and the development team. Quickly prototype multiple ideas and adapt them to the evolving needs of the project.
  • Contribute to the creation of immersive worlds by designing mood boards, color palettes, and atmospheric elements. Help establish the overall visual identity of the game.
  • Maintain detailed documentation of designs to guide the production team during development. Create art assets for promotional materials if needed.
  • Provide creative solutions to visual challenges, ensuring designs are functional, appealing, and fit the gameplay mechanics.
